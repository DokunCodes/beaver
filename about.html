Who we are 

Beaver Home Maintenance Company is a young and innovative organization with proudly Nigerian roots. We have melded our years of professional experience in construction and IT into a bespoke model that brings home maintenance in Nigeria into the digital age by making it easy to access through our app based service.

From our years of experience in providing building solutions across Nigeria, we have come to understand the frustration faced by many Nigerians when dealing with artisans. At Beaver Home Maintenance Service Company, we deal with these issues on your behalf making sure you never have to worry about overpriced labour and inefficient workmanship.

Against this backdrop, we have put at your fingertips, a team of professionals and experts that have the experience and skills needed to provide all your home/corporate repair and maintenance solutions in plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, air conditioning systems, roof work, felting, masonry, painting and other related services.

We appreciate the productivity and peace of mind a well maintained domicile brings and we provide only the highest quality service to all our clients.

From replacing a light bulb to fixing your roof, we proudly guarantee the satisfaction that comes when you trust Beaver.
