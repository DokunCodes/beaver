How does Beaver work?
Log on to the Beaver website or download our app from Google Play Store and iOS App Store; register, pay and call us ANYTIME you need to. We fix or replace all items covered by your plan at no additional cost to you. Your payment covers you for 12 months and you never have to make additional out of pocket payments to anyone for materials or labour.
How do I make payment?
Register and subscribe to one of our plans by downloading Beaver Home app or visiting www.beaver.ng Pay into any of the bank accounts provided using your unique reference code to confirm your registration and subscription. Your payment will be acknowledged within 24 hours of payment confirmation.
How do I notify Beaver of damages?
Call us between the hours of 9am to 5pm from Monday to Friday and we will dispatch a Beaver home manager to you within 24 hours from your time of call.
Is my payment a one off?
Yes. Once you have paid your subscription, you do not need to pay any additional money for the Beaver Service. All monies are paid to our Beaver bank accounts.
